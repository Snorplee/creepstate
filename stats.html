  • • Clear resolution with presidential resignation
  • • Peak: Senate hearings (May-Nov 1973)
  • • Duration: 26 months total

📝 Monica Lewinsky Pattern (1998)

  • • Explosive initial revelation in January 1998
  • • Sustained high coverage through impeachment
  • • Clear legal resolution with Senate acquittal
  • • Peak: Impeachment proceedings (Dec 1998)
  • • Duration: 12 months intensive coverage

🏫 Sandy Hook Response Pattern (2012-2013)

  • • Immediate massive media response
  • • Congressional hearings within 6 weeks
  • • Limited federal legislative resolution

  • • Duration: 16 months active legislation efforts
